1760 - The writing of History Slavyanobulgarskaya




The writing of Slavyanobulgarskaya History by Paisii Hilendarski is considered to be the peculiar beginning
of the Bulgarian Revival. The history was written in the period 1760-1762. Its first transcript is the work
of Sophrony of Vratsa (1765). The work of the monk Paisius of Mount Athos is rightly pointed out as an
important impetus for the formation of the Bulgarian national self-consciousness. In the long years of
slavery, Bulgarians lost their sense of community, the homeland for most of them has shrunk to their own
small town or village. The Bulgarians had a great history and great kings, but they did not know about
their past. Structurally, the History consists of several parts - 2 prefaces, several chapters, which set
out various historical events, a chapter on Slavic teachers, a chapter on Bulgarian saints and anafterword.
