1940 - The rescue of the Bulgarian Jews




Include this episode of native history as a kind of bonus to the rankings made so far.
With this act, which is the work of the joint efforts of the entire Bulgarian people,
together with the church and a number of political figures and intellectuals who raise their voices in
defense of Bulgarian Jews. The events in Bulgaria in the period 1940-1944, connected with the fate of
the Jews, are observed today mostly as a successful attempt to save them from deportation to the Nazi
death camps. But that's not all. The strong reaction against the anti-Jewish law, the attacks on the
very philosophy of persecution of people on ethnic and religious grounds, the disagreement with the
unconstitutional restriction of the rights of any citizen say that these events have a much wider
scope than salvation itself, namely that they are directed against anti-Semitism in general.
